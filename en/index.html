Back to the Future: 1918

Past Edition

On 9-10 June, Tech Park hosted the Data Visualization Hackathon - Back to the Future: 1918. Designers, developers, communication specialists, history researchers, journalists and people who are interested in the data worked together to visualise the historic data of the past 100 years and bring history to life.

This was the second edition of the Data Visualization Hackathon, which enables participants to create different kinds of digital tools, increase their knowledge in the field of data communication, get acquainted with modern technologies, meet people who are also interested in these topics and work with experienced mentors to develop your products.
